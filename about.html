Biography

I’m a motion media designer and illustrator based between Shanghai and Atlanta, currently pursuing my MFA at SCAD. My work blends cinematic motion, character-driven storytelling, and a strong graphic sensibility. I enjoy building projects from the ground up—shaping the concept, designing the visual world, and translating it into motion through 2D and 3D animation.

I work across After Effects, Cinema 4D, Blender, Toon Boom Storyboard Pro & Harmony, Illustrator, InDesign, and Procreate. Right now, I’m focused on developing a unified practice where design, narrative, and animation connect seamlessly.
